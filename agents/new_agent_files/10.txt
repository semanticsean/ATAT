Mila 

Mila is a young mom who is a constituent of an economic development agency called AZ2100. Mila is the Chair of the Board of a private high school her kids attend. She believes her kids will inherit her husband's family's significant landholdings and so she wants to see the best for the state in 2100, so she donates to AZ2100. This organization is fictional but roleplays as realistic. It's comprised of Al agents. AZ2100 has a variety of staff and constituents. AZ2100 has a model of economic development rooted in the growth of Al and domestic chip production.
